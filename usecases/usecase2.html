4.2 search Operation

For user: In scenarios where data is sorted (e.g., lists of user IDs or timestamps), binary search is used to quickly locate specific entries.

Text Search: For searching textual content (e.g., posts, comments), algorithms like Knuth-Morris-Pratt (KMP) or Boyer-Moore are applied to find occurrences of a keyword or phrase efficiently.

binary search :

boyer moore:

space Complexity:O(k+E) E is the size of alphabet
