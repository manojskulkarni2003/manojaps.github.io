Introduction - Working on Facebook

Facebook is a social media platform founded by Mark Zuckerberg and his college roommates in 2004. It allows users to connect with friends, family, and communities online.

The basic goals of Facebook include:

  • Facilitating communication and sharing of information between users.
  • Building communities and fostering connections globally.
  • Providing a platform for businesses and organizations to reach their audiences.
  • Offering tools and features for personalized user experiences and content discovery.

Facebook has evolved into one of the largest social networking sites, influencing how people interact, share, and engage with content online.
